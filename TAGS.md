# Tags

The canonical tag set for this blog. Keep tags to this list — when something genuinely doesn't fit, add it here first with a definition, don't invent one-offs in a post.

Tags fall into three groups: **functional** (drive site behavior), **format** (what kind of writing it is), and **topic** (what it's about). A post usually carries 2–4 tags: at most one format tag, plus the topic tags that actually apply. Most posts also carry a functional tag.

## Functional (behavior, not content)

These trigger layout features — don't use them as content labels.

| Tag | Meaning |
|-----|---------|
| `ai-generated` | Written with substantial AI involvement. Pairs with `ai_conversation_url`, which renders the blue AI disclaimer. |
| `in-review` | Work in progress / not finished. Renders the orange "in review" disclaimer. Remove when the piece is done. |

## Format

What kind of writing it is. Pick at most one.

| Tag | Meaning |
|-----|---------|
| `poetry` | Poems. |
| `diy` | Hands-on build / how-to with materials and steps (e.g. the pipe bookshelf). |

## Topic

What the piece is about. Use as many as genuinely apply.

### Technology
| Tag | Meaning |
|-----|---------|
| `coding` | Hands-on software, web, and mobile development — CSS, Flutter, architecture, site-building. |
| `ai` | AI as a subject: AI-assisted coding, AI agents, AI hardware, the AI age. (Distinct from the `ai-generated` functional tag.) |

### Jewish thought
| Tag | Meaning |
|-----|---------|
| `chassidus` | Chabad / Hasidic philosophy and the inner dimension of Torah — Tanya, the Alter Rebbe, mysticism, faith-and-reason in a Chassidic key. |
| `judaism` | Jewish life, law, identity, community, and prayer — the broader bucket for Jewish topics that aren't specifically Chassidic philosophy. |

### Ideas

### Culture & society
| Tag | Meaning |

### Life
| Tag | Meaning |

## Notes for adding new content

- Reach for an existing tag before adding a new one. `culture`/`politics` and `philosophy`/`ethics` are deliberately broad buckets — most cultural-criticism and idea pieces fit one of them.
- `chassidus` vs `judaism`: use `chassidus` for inner-Torah / Chabad philosophy; use `judaism` for practice, law, identity, and community. A piece can carry both.
- `diy` and `language` are occasional tags — fine to leave a post as the only one under them.
- If you genuinely need a new tag, add a row above with a one-line definition so the set stays meaningful.
